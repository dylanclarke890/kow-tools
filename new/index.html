Welcome to KOW Tools!

This is a free for use website which has a variety of tools for the mobile game Kiss of War. This site's sole intent is to provide tools to the players of KOW to save you time when trying to accurately work out how much of the various items you have in game.

Resource Calculator

This calculator is part one of three separate pages that you can complete and see a summary of at the end. This page will add up how many of each resource type you have as well as break down the totals depending which sections you fill in.

Speedup Calculator

This calculator is part two of three separate pages that you can complete and see a summary of at the end. This page will add up and display (in days, hours, minutes and seconds) how many of each speedup type you have as well as break down the totals depending which sections you fill in.

Troop Calculator

This calculator is part three of three separate pages that you can complete and see a summary of at the end. This page will show you how much it will cost and how long it will take to train the amount of troops you need, depending on the troop level you want, the amount you can train at one time and the time it takes for you to train that amount.

Officer Calculator

This calculator is a standalone tool used for adding up total experience points and total action points, which can be done by filling in the appropriate sections. Once the XP calculator section has been filled in, you can use the third section to see if you have enough experience points to level your officer up to the level you want, depending on the rarity and level they're starting from.
